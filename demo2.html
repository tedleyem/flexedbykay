FAQ

What is Flexed all about?

Flexed is your ultimate destination for yoga, stretching, and flex therapy. We offer personalized programs to enhance your flexibility and well-being.

Is yoga suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our instructors are skilled at guiding beginners through the practice of yoga, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience.

How often should I stretch?

We recommend incorporating stretching into your daily routine to improve flexibility and prevent injury.

What are the benefits of flex therapy?

Flex therapy helps alleviate muscle tension, improve posture, and enhance overall flexibility and mobility.

Can I combine yoga and flex therapy?

Yes, combining yoga and flex therapy can provide a holistic approach to improving flexibility and overall well-being.
